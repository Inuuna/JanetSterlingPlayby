Janet Sterling
Born in 1887 has Janet Sterling, the daughter of the leader to a small Asphodelian gang called the "Gouged Eye" , by the name of Skeekh Sterling. She soon grow up into a petty pocket thief by 10. by the time she was 13 she would constantly be a lookout on local burglaries often in her father's allies neighbourhod, she would soon anger him. by the time she grew to be 16 her father would turn very sick, and word of it spread like wildfire across all of Asphodel, other gangs would be growing impatient to incorporate the rest of the Gouged Eye, while the members including the sterling family were worried about who will take on leading the Gouged Eye to survive towards on. one day Skeekh Sterling called Janet to his study telling her that her mother were Magi from Esshar, and that he is sending her to Esshar to learn about her gift and take on to forming her own division of bandits to eventually return to Asphodel with to take on leadership of the Gouged Eye.
